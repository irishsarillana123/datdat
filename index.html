Arnis is a Filipino martial art and the national sport of the Philippines, characterized by weapon-based fighting, primarily with sticks, though it also includes the use of swords and empty-hand techniques like joint locks and grappling.

Violations in Arnis, also called fouls, include striking or thrusting to forbidden areas like the groin and back of the head, using both hands for striking, excessive contact, prolonged clinching, pushing, holding an opponent's stick, going out of the playing area, and not engaging with the opponent.
